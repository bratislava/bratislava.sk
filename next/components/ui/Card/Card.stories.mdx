# Card

Card

## Props

Card accepts custom Props that are as follows:

- <b>className</b>: modifies or extends the styles for the component
- <b>hasButton</b>: has button or not
- <b>children</b>: content of the component
- <b>buttonVariant</b>: variant of button, default or circle
- <b>buttonContent</b>: text of button
