## Props

ContentWrapper accepts custom Props that are as follows:

- <b>className</b>: modifies or extends the styles for the component
- <b>title</b>: title of the component
- <b>children</b>: content of the component.
- <b>description</b>: description of the component.
- <b>topper</b>: component, that is positioned at the top of the WaveWrapper.
