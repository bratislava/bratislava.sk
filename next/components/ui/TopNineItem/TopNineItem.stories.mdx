## Props

Tag accepts custom Props that are as follows:

- <b>className</b>: modifies or extends the styles for the component
- <b>icon</b>: icon above content
- <b>title</b>: Title of the Section
- <b>href</b>: pathname of the linked page
- <b>linkTitle</b>: Title of the link
